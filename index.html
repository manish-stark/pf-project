Welcome to laundry Wallah / Laundry Mart

A laundry wallah, or dhobiwallah, is the traditional Indian term for a washerman who offers laundry services, but "Laundrywala" (as a brand) now signifies a modern, tech-driven company providing organized, doorstep laundry and dry-cleaning with apps for booking, tracking, and digital payments, blending traditional garment care with convenience for busy professionals and families.
